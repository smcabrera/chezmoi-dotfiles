# Example Peer Review - Rihanna

This example demonstrates how to write a strong peer review that is specific, balanced, and constructive.

---

## Impact

Rihanna's contributions over the past six months have had a strong impact on the team's success and ability to meet goals.

- **Improving Processes:** She introduced a new interview scheduling system that reduced delays and improved communication, saving time and creating a better experience for candidates and hiring managers.
- **Collaborative Success:** During the virtual career fair, Rihanna coordinated logistics, brought in creative ideas, and rallied the team. Her efforts contributed to a 20% increase in qualified applicants.
- **Fostering Team Growth:** Rihanna supports others by sharing insights and solutions. For example, she helped me navigate a tricky scheduling conflict while teaching a new approach for future situations.
- **Resilience in Challenges:** She stays organized and positive under pressure, quickly adapting to unexpected changes during hiring projects.

---

## Development & Goal Setting

Rihanna is an outstanding contributor, and a few areas for growth could further enhance her impact:

1. **Delegation:** She sometimes takes on too much herself. Delegating more would lighten her load and allow others to contribute.
2. **Big-Picture Focus:** While detail-oriented, balancing that with broader objectives could increase her impact on larger projects.
3. **Proactive Updates:** Sharing regular updates during projects would help the team stay aligned and know when to step in.

---

## Additional Information (Optional)

Rihanna exemplifies **User Interviews' core values:**

- **#People-First:** She ensures candidates and colleagues feel valued, like scheduling extra prep sessions for nervous candidates.
- **#Stay-Curious:** She continuously looks for improvements, such as suggesting a new tool to streamline candidate communication.
- **#Welcome-People-As-They-Are:** She champions inclusivity, making all candidates and team members feel respected and welcome.

---

## Additional Anonymous Feedback (Optional)

Occasionally, Rihanna hesitates to adapt quickly to sudden changes in direction. Providing mentorship or training on agility in fast-paced situations could help her feel more confident and make transitions smoother.

---

## What Makes This Review Effective

**Specific examples:** Not "good communicator" but "introduced a new interview scheduling system that reduced delays"

**Quantified impact:** "20% increase in qualified applicants" makes the impact concrete

**Balanced:** Acknowledges strengths while offering constructive growth areas

**Actionable growth areas:** Each development point is specific and explains how it would help

**Values connection:** Shows concrete examples of how values are lived, not just stated

**Constructive tone:** Growth areas are framed as opportunities, not criticisms

**Observable behaviors:** Focuses on what she *does* (coordinates logistics, shares insights) not just qualities
